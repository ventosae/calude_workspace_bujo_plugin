Run the full weekly review using the `weekly-review` skill.

Read: `bujo/INDEX.md`, `bujo/capture/inbox.md`, all daily logs in `bujo/daily/` from the past 7 days, `bujo/WEEKLY_LOG.md`, `bujo/WAITING_FOR.md`, `bujo/SOMEDAY_MAYBE.md`, `bujo/FUTURE_LOG.md`.

Follow all 6 steps in order:
1. COLLECT — process inbox to zero
2. REVIEW THE WEEK — read daily logs, surface incomplete tasks and patterns
3. REFLECT — four Stoic questions (what went well, what failed, what was out of my control, what am I grateful for)
4. MIGRATE — kill or carry every open task
5. LOOK AHEAD — pull next week's calendar via Google Calendar MCP, set 3 weekly intentions
6. MEMENTO MORI — one sentence: what would I change if this was my last week?

Do not skip steps. End with the three intentions for next week written in the new WEEKLY_LOG.
